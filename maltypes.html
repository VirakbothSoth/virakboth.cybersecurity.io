Welcome to Malwares, These are the attackers of your device. Sometimes they steal cookies, passwords, files & others. This page should teach you what each type of malware does & how to avoid it.



Types of Malware



1. Trojan Horse




Trojan Horse (or Trojans) is a type of malware that pretends to be an app, game, or other forms of program. Once in your computer, they steal passwords, cookies & any other of your information.


How do we avoid it? Don't fall for any strange websites that say something like "Free Minecraft Download" or anything that claims to give a free service of a paid service



2. Worms




Worms is a type of malware that spreads with any means, such as Spam Email. once they have a host computer they normally corrupt files of a computer & continue spreading by sending bad emails to other computers, transfer itself to other computers of the same wifi network, etc.


How do we avoid it? Stay away from anything that looks suspious & get an antivirus in case you are using public wifi



3. Ransomware




Ransomware is a type of malware that locks your files, personal info, etc. They require a randomized key that is used to unlock your files. In return, they ask for money or cryptocurrency.


How do we avoid it? Most free antiviruses can't stop a ransomware attack, so stay away from anything suspious as usual. Always run a scan on a file/folder you think is a ransomware program.



4. Spyware




Spyware is a type of malware that secretly steals information without you knowing it. This data can range from credit cards, passwords, just about anything you have inputted before


How do we avoid it? Some antiviruses can automatically detect it, but if it happens to not find it open your task manager and find any suspious applications.



5. Rootkits




Rootkit is a type of stealth malware that camouflages itself into System Files to not be detected, It can manipulate & control system functions, give control to cybercriminals, etc.


How do we avoid it? Advanced antiviruses can detect rootkits if they look too suspious. Don't grant any permissions to any suspious programs
